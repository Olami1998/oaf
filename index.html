About Ola Abiola Foundation

Founded in 2010 by Ola Abiola (MisterSlim), our foundation was born from the understanding of the unique challenges single mothers face in raising children while providing for their families.

What began as a small community initiative has grown into a nationally recognized organization providing comprehensive support services to single mothers across the country.

Our Mission

To empower single mothers through education, resources, and community support.

Our Vision

A world where every single mother has the tools and support needed to thrive.
